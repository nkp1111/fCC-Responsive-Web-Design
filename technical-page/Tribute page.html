Leonardo da Vinci

Short overview of Leonardo da Vinvi life

  • Birth : 15 April 1452
  • Known for : Painting, Drawing, Engineering, Science, Sculpture, Architecture
  • His fame initially rested on his achievements as a painter.
  • Paintings : Most famous ones are :
    1. Mona Lisa (often regarded as the world's most famous painting)
    2. The Last Supper (most reproduced religious painting of all time)
    3. Vitruvian Man (regarded as a cultural icon)
    4. and many more...
  • Discovery : He made substantial discoveries in
    1. anatomy
    2. civil engineering
    3. hydrodynamics
    4. geology
    5. optics
    6. tribology
  • Death : At age of 67, 2 May 1519

Leonardo is widely regarded to have been a genius who epitomized the Renaissance humanist ideal, and his collective works compose a contribution to later generations of artists.
